## Recording guardrails

- Do not select or describe hidden truth that is not present in the current character's owned memories or beliefs.
- Keep the mode pill visible when stating that the run is deterministic Recorded data.
- Pause only after the current completed turn appears in the timeline.
- End on the restored turn-zero checkpoint to demonstrate reproducibility.
